White Paper v1.0

The idea behind GoodTransit is to bring easy access to all forms of transportation tom city livers, travelers, and developer.

The app will allow users to find routes, plan trips, and discover cities all while easily traveling the city. Bringing features such as previous routes, smart routes, exploration around the city, fare prices, ticket purchasing, and more.

Concept

In basic terms, the main function of GoodTransit would be for people, mostly city livers, to easily get access to the most efficient and cheap ways of transport to work, the store, sporting events, and more.

Ways of accessing this information will be through automated codebases and data collectors. Projects such as GTFS make this possible and easy to access.

Products

Our main product would be the route planning and route generator. Althoguh, these will not be the only products. Things such as trip planning, city exploration, and developer APIs will be other products offered by our service.
