GoodTransit is a transportation app bringing direction, accessibility, and time savings to all 300 million Americans living in urban settings and the tens of millions more traveling to these areas.

Bringing buses, subways, trains, planes, boats, bikes, scooters, Ubers, and sooo much more into one app.

Concept

The concept behind GoodTransit is one app with all the functions needed for transportation in your life whether it is for traveling, commuting, or fun. Features like passports, IDs, and planning in-app makes traveling abroad and domestically easy.

The four main features of the app are (1) route planning, (2) city exploring, (3) passport/ID for travel, and (4) ticket/card and airline purchasing.

Other Services

Other services would include developer APIs and GTFS/GBFS/MDS data dumps. These would include route finding and city data APIs so developers can implement transit into their apps and websites.
